Contrast Therapy Benefits

By alternating between hot and cold water during a single session, Drycuzzi enables contrast therapy—a proven technique used by professional athletes and physical therapists. Cold water constricts blood vessels to reduce inflammation and swelling, while hot water dilates them to increase blood flow and promote healing. This combination accelerates recovery and provides superior therapeutic results compared to single-temperature treatments.
